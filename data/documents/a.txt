Quarterly Financial Report — Q3 2025
======================================
Prepared by: Finance Department
Date: October 15, 2025

Summary
-------
Total revenue for Q3 2025 reached $4.2M, representing a 12% increase
over Q2 2025 and a 34% increase year-over-year. Operating expenses
remained flat at $2.1M, resulting in a net profit of $2.1M.

Key Metrics
-----------
- Revenue: $4,200,000
- COGS: $1,050,000
- Gross Margin: 75%
- Operating Expenses: $2,100,000
- Net Profit: $2,100,000
- EBITDA: $2,450,000
- Cash on Hand: $8,700,000

Department Breakdown
-------------------
- Engineering: $1,200,000 (28.6% of revenue)
- Sales & Marketing: $800,000 (19.0% of revenue)
- Operations: $400,000 (9.5% of revenue)
- R&D: $600,000 (14.3% of revenue)
- Admin: $500,000 (11.9% of revenue)

Outlook for Q4
--------------
We project continued growth with expected revenue of $4.5M.
New product launches in the pipeline may accelerate growth further.
