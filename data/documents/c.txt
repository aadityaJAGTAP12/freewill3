1. Code of Conduct
------------------
All employees are expected to maintain professional standards
of behavior. This includes respectful communication, ethical
decision-making, and compliance with all applicable laws.

2. Remote Work Policy
---------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 5 days per week with manager
approval. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Eastern Time. All meetings should have video enabled.

3. Security Guidelines
----------------------
- Use strong passwords (minimum 16 characters)
- Enable 2FA on all company accounts
- Report security incidents immediately to IT
- Never share credentials via email or chat
- Lock your screen when away from your desk

4. Leave and Benefits
---------------------
- Paid Time Off: 20 days per year
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid
- Health Insurance: 100% employer-covered
- 401(k) matching: 4% of salary
